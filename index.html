Zero's Initiative

Vision

We as a community of industry professionals, have a lot of material with us which we would like to share with each and every college student, industry professional for free. Our initiative and the content that we have should reach every single college, in every district of India. To spread awareness about the same and provide career guidance is our goal. Our aim is to take on as many students as possible, to nurture them and provide them with direction and the resources necessary to take them to the next level.

Mission

To provide education for free for every student and industry professional working in the realm of IT. We provide the WHAT, WHY and HOW factor to do great in the field of IT.
